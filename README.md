# 📘 The Uncertainty Algorithm

### Exploring the Beautiful World of Quantum Mechanics

---

## 📖 About the Book

**The Uncertainty Algorithm** is a beginner-friendly introduction to quantum mechanics that explains complex concepts in a simple and intuitive way.  

Written from a student’s perspective, this book breaks down the mysterious behavior of the quantum world into clear, step-by-step explanations.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔹 Simple explanations of complex quantum concepts  
- 🔹 Step-by-step learning approach  
- 🔹 Real-world examples and analogies  
- 🔹 Covers core topics of modern physics  
- 🔹 Designed especially for students and beginners  

---

## 🧠 Topics Covered

- Introduction to Quantum Mechanics  
- Wave-Particle Duality  
- Energy Quantization  
- Wave Function  
- Uncertainty Principle  
- Quantum Superposition  
- Quantum Tunneling  
- Pauli Exclusion Principle  
- Quantum Entanglement  
- Quantum Decoherence  
- Quantum Teleportation  
- Entanglement Swapping  
- Principle of Causality
